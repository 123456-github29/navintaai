ADD A CINEMATIC CLAPPERBOARD INTRO (DO NOT REPLACE HERO)

Create a full-screen overlay that plays ONCE per session:
	1.	Screen loads → silence, stillness, focus
	2.	Minimal SVG clapperboard fades in center
	3.	Clapper arm opens slowly (precision, weight)
	4.	As user scrolls or after 1.5s:
	•	arm closes with a crisp snap
	•	micro camera shake (1–2px)
	•	subtle soundless impact moment
	5.	Overlay dissolves into thin lines / particles
	6.	Underlying hero animates in as if the scene just started

This is the “Action!” moment of the site.

Remove overlay from DOM after animation completes.

Every section should feel like a new scene.
