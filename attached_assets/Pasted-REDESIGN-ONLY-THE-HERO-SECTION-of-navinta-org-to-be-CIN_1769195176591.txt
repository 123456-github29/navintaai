REDESIGN ONLY THE HERO SECTION of navinta.org to be CINEMATIC, MEMORABLE, and DIRECTOR-LEVEL.
The hero must feel like the opening shot of a film, not a SaaS landing page.

This is NOT a layout task. This is a MOMENT.

========================================================
CONCEPT: “THE FIRST TAKE”
========================================================

Navinta is an AI director. The hero section is the moment someone yells “Action”.

The hero should feel like:
- entering a film set
- the beginning of creation
- confidence, control, intention
- cinematic, calm, premium

========================================================
HERO STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)
========================================================

FULL-VIEWPORT HERO (100vh)
Center stage composition, nothing cramped, nothing busy.

Layered structure (very important):
1. Background atmosphere
2. Clapperboard animation (main character)
3. Hero headline (big, cinematic)
4. Supporting subline (quiet, confident)
5. CTA (only one primary)
6. Subtle motion details (life, not noise)

========================================================
BACKGROUND (FIX THE BORING FEEL)
========================================================

Background must have DEPTH and ATMOSPHERE, not flat color.

Implement:
- Soft blue-white gradient wash (like Vyro)
- Very large radial glow behind center (like a spotlight)
- Subtle film grain/noise texture (VERY faint)
- Slight vignette at edges
- Slow drifting light (opacity animation, 12–18s loop)

It should feel like a lit soundstage, not a website.

========================================================
CLAPPERBOARD ANIMATION (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
========================================================

Create a 3D-ish (or 2.5D) clapperboard in the center.

Animation sequence:
1. On page load:
   - clapperboard spins in slowly from depth (scale 0.6 → 1)
   - slight perspective tilt
   - soft shadow below (grounding)
2. It pauses for 300ms
3. CLAP:
   - top bar snaps shut
   - small motion blur
   - subtle sound-wave ripple (visual only)
   - entire scene reacts with micro shake (very subtle)
4. As it snaps, the headline fades/slides in perfectly timed
5. Clapperboard then drifts upward and becomes decorative (top-center)

This instantly communicates:
“Navinta is a director, not a tool.”

========================================================
HEADLINE (MAKE IT FEEL IMPORTANT)
========================================================

Huge, bold, cinematic type.

Example structure (NOT gradient text, NOT colored words):
“Press record.  
Navinta directs the rest.”

OR

“From idea to action.  
Your AI director.”

Rules:
- No awkward accent coloring (remove blue words)
- Use hierarchy, not color, for emphasis
- First line bold black
- Second line slightly lighter gray
- Tight line-height
- Centered, confident

========================================================
SUBLINE (QUIET POWER)
========================================================

One sentence only.
Soft gray.
Feels like a movie tagline.

Example:
“Plan, record, and publish videos with the confidence of a professional director.”

========================================================
CTA (CONFIDENT, NOT SALESY)
========================================================

One primary CTA only:
“Start your first take”

Style:
- Pill
- Calm blue
- Slight glow on hover (very subtle)
- Appears AFTER the headline (staggered animation)

========================================================
SCROLL TRANSITION (IMPORTANT)
========================================================

When user scrolls:
- Clapperboard subtly moves upward and fades
- Hero content scales down slightly
- Next section (How it works) slides up like a new scene

This should feel like:
Scene 1 → Scene 2
Not “scrolling down a page”

========================================================
ANIMATION RULES (IMPORTANT)
========================================================

Use GSAP timeline:
- Load sequence (staggered)
- Clapper snap synced with headline
- ScrollTrigger for exit

DO NOT:
- Bounce
- Rotate wildly
- Use neon glows
- Add excessive blur
- Add random floating shapes

========================================================
FIX THE “AWKWARD ACCENT” PROBLEM
========================================================

Remove:
- Blue words in headlines
- Highlighted phrases
- Mixed emphasis styles

Instead:
- Use spacing
- Use line breaks
- Use weight
- Use motion timing

Professional sites use rhythm, not color, to emphasize.

FINAL CHECK
========================================================

After implementation, the hero should make users think:
“Oh. This is different.”
“This feels serious.”
“This feels expensive.”
“I want to try this.”
