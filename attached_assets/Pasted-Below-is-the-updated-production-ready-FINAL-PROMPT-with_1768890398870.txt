# **FINAL PROMPT**

You are a **senior product designer + full-stack engineer** building a **pro-level AI video content platform** called **Navinta**.

Navinta helps founders, creators, and brands **plan, record, edit, and publish short-form video content** using AI-generated content plans and a guided in-browser recording experience (**Director Mode**).

Build this as a **production-ready, responsive web app**, desktop-first for recording, fully functional on tablet and mobile.

---

## **PRODUCT NAME**

**Navinta**
**Tagline:** *Plan once. Record with clarity. Publish with confidence.*

---

## **PRODUCT VISION**

Navinta removes friction from content creation by:

* Turning brand inputs into a structured content calendar
* Guiding users shot-by-shot while recording
* Making editing fast, intuitive, and repeatable
* Centralizing video management, scheduling, and analytics

This should feel like **Linear × Notion × CapCut × Stripe Dashboard**.

---

## **DESIGN PRINCIPLES**

* Professional, confident, calm
* Creator-first UX (large controls, clear instructions)
* Minimal UI with powerful depth
* Strong visual hierarchy
* Dark & light mode
* Subtle motion and micro-interactions
* Clear progress and completion states everywhere

---

## **GLOBAL TECH & UI STACK**

* **Framework:** React + TypeScript
* **Routing:** App Router style
* **Styling:** TailwindCSS
* **UI Primitives:** Radix / Headless UI
* **Icons:** Lucide
* **Animation:** Framer Motion
* **Video:** MediaRecorder API
* **Drag & Drop:** DnD Kit
* **Charts:** Recharts
* **Auth:** Email + OAuth ready
* **Storage:** Signed URLs for video playback
* **AI Hooks:** Stubbed AI services for plans, scripts, captions
